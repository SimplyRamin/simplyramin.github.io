ML Based Instagram's Influencer Ranker

June 2021
-
August 2021

In this project, I trained a model for ranking the influencers on Instagram. I asked three experts in digital marketing with many years of experience to score the training dataset of influencers which all are shuffled and de-identified to remove the biases towards them. I created a pipeline based on Instagram's API to extract features to rank influencers, some of these features are not available in Instagram mobile or web application, such as category, etc, and some of these features are calculated based on other features such as frequency of sharing posts, etc. also, a logging mechanism designed for this pipeline so after deployment of this model, a dataset for retraining and discovering far-out influencers.

#Bekhatereman Campaign Data-Driven Analysis

November 2020
-
January 2021

In this project, I analyzed the #Bekhatereman campaign (a CSR Campaign for the Official Distributor of Mitsubishi Motors in Iran) data with the Data Science approach. The goal of this project was to assess the effectiveness of this campaign which was held on Instagram based on advertising cost and sentiment analysis. In this project, I have used the CRISP-DM methodology. Every aspect of this project was implemented from scratch by me the most important section is data gathering which I designed and developed an Instagram bot to retrieve the data necessary for this endeavor. In this project, I have trained more than 250 models with different algorithms and hyperparameters.
